Who We Are

Built After the War,
Built for the Future

In 1993, as Liberia was healing from conflict, Don Bosco Technical High School opened its doors with a simple promise: no student should be left on the street. We offer quality education at minimal cost, in the tradition of St. John Bosco — the Italian priest who dedicated his life to youth.
